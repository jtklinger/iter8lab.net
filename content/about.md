I'm Jeremy Klinger, an infrastructure engineer who builds and documents homelab environments.

iter8lab is where I write about the tools, architectures, and decisions behind running self-hosted infrastructure across two domains -- a production homelab (lab.towerbancorp.com) and a family services platform (ourhomeport.com).

Topics include mesh VPN architectures, DNS design, container orchestration, backup strategies, and the occasional deep dive into whatever problem I'm solving that week.

The infrastructure runs on Rocky Linux, Podman, Ceph, and a growing collection of self-hosted services managed as code through Git.
